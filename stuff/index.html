Other Stuff

Here's some other stuff I've made that may or may not be interesting.

  • The Blank of Blank - This is the McDonald's of dumb websites.
  • Buttsbot - A Twitch bot that randomly repeats what chat users say while replacing random syllables with "butt". It's in over 10,000 Twitch channels! Credit goes to pinguino for originally creating the bot, I simply wrote the current version of it.
  • ClipArtBot - A Twitch bot that displays a random image of a given keyword entered by a chat member. It can also automatically replace textures in GameCube and Wii games with the Dolphin emulator. It can even be used as a MIDI jukebox! A very old version is available for download, maybe eventually I'll set up the current version so that other people can use it.
  • Clip Art or Stock Photo? - A paper I wrote in college about using neural networks to determine whether a given image is clip art or a stock photo.
  • RollerCoaster Tycoon Challenges - A program that randomly selects challenges to make your RollerCoaster Tycoon experience that much more challenging.
  • SRB2 Kart: Supermarket Sweep Zone - A custom track I made for Sonic Robo Blast 2 Kart where you drive around a supermarket.
