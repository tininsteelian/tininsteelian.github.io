Other Stuff

Here's some other stuff I've made that may or may not be interesting.

  • The Blank of Blank - This is the McDonald's of dumb websites.
  • Buttsbot - A Twitch bot that randomly repeats what chat users say while replacing random syllables with "butt". It's in over 1,000 Twitch channels! Credit goes to pinguino for originally creating the bot, I simply wrote the current version of it.
  • ClipArtBot - A Twitch bot that displays a random image of a given keyword entered by a chat member. It can also automatically replace textures in GameCube and Wii games with the Dolphin emulator. It's even a MIDI jukebox! A very old version is available for download, maybe eventually I'll set up the current version so that other people can use it.
  • Clip Art or Stock Photo? - A paper I wrote in college about using neural networks to determine whether a given image is clip art or a stock photo.
  • RollerCoaster Tycoon Challenges - A program that randomly selects challenges to make your RollerCoaster Tycoon experience that much more challenging.
  • TinBot - A Discord bot that hosts trivia matches. It can also suggest what food to eat, provide motivational quotes, and acquire information using WolframAlpha. There isn't currently a download link for it, sorry.
