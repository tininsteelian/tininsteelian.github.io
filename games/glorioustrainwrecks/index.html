Glorious Trainwrecks Games

These games were made for various Klik of the Month and related game jams.
They mostly use assets from Klik & Play and random MIDIs from various sources.
Click on the icons to play!


Jewels vs. Train Cars: The Ultimate Battle

Released on June 3, 2017

Show the red car that train cars are superior to jewels!

Made for KotM #104

Spin Spinner 1-25

Released on November 27-28, 2015

A series of 25 games I made where you move a spinning object to the goal. I can't host all 25 on this site, so the link goes to a Glorious Trainwrecks page where you can download them.

Made for KotM #100

Halloween Bounce House

Released on October 18, 2014

Avoid the reproducing vampires until there are enough of them to cause a massive chain reaction of bounciness and chaos.

Made for KotM #88

The Oregano Trail: 6th Edition

Released on January 23, 2014

An Oregon Trail fan game. As you traverse the Oregon Trail, you'll face dreaded obstacles such as flowers, small rocks, and elephants.

Made for KotM #79

Klik and Stacker

Released on January 18, 2014

Build the tallest tower possible by stacking pieces from Klik and Play on top of each other. This game is the prequel to Everyday Stacker.

Made for KotM #79
