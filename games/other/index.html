Other Games

These are miscellaneous games, most of which are only available as downloads.


Spin Spinner (app)

Last Developed on May 11, 2016

No relation to the Spin Spinner series of Glorious Trainwrecks games. This was going to be an Android app, but it was cancelled. Spin the spinner to find out the answer to any yes-or-no question.

Hoverboard Penguin

Last Developed on March 12, 2015

An unfinished game, kind of like Super Monkey Ball but in 2D. The first world is playable.

Incredible Automobile Racing Simulator

Finished on May 23, 2014

A project I made for a programming class in high school, made in Just BASIC. Steer the car and build as much speed as possible before crashing! This game is the prequel to Tinzone Driving Simulator 2015.5.

Fishy Mach II

Finished on October 27, 2010

Shoot the evil Fishy Mach II to save the day! This is an Atari 2600 game, so you'll need an Atari 2600 emulator to play it.

Normberry Hunt

Finished on January 13, 2010

Move around the screen as Norm to collect the Normberries! This is an Atari 2600 game, so you'll need an Atari 2600 emulator to play it.

Tin's Old Games

From 2005-2015

A compilation of more than 50 games I made in The Games Factory and Multimedia Fusion when I was (mostly) a kid. All of the games have been converted to executables that work on modern Windows PCs.
