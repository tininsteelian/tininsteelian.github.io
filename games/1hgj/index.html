One Hour Game Jam Games

These games were made in one hour (usually a bit longer than that, shhh...) for various One Hour Game Jams.
Click on the icons to play!


Shoe Shoo

Released on January 25, 2020

Some shoes don't want to be worn...

Made for 1HGJ #248: You can run, but you can't hide

Taco Tom Can Dab!

Released on January 18, 2020

But only to the Maple Leaf Rag, apparently.

Made for 1HGJ #247: oof

Four Drill Bits

Released on January 4, 2020

A new law says that having more than four drill bits in your possession at any time is illegal. More drill bits are landing on your table, so you must drill them away with one of the four drill bits you already have.

Made for 1HGJ #245: 4-Bits

Cactus Fork

Released on December 21, 2019

Operate the futuristic Cactus Fork and pick up as many pieces of bacon as possible before spiraling out of control.

Made for 1HGJ #243: Cactus

Do Not Drive Forwards

Released on November 30, 2019

Set the fastest lap you can on the track, but you'll be disqualified if you drive forwards, so don't do that.

Made for 1HGJ #240: Go Back

No Rats Allowed!

Released on November 23, 2019

Click the rats (black borders) to remove them! They're not allowed to be around the mice (white borders). Don't hurt any of the mice!

Made for 1HGJ #239: Mouse Only

Sandwich Maker

Released on November 16, 2019

Make the sandwich with the most ingredients possible! Press the left and right arrow keys to move the plate. Press both keys simultaneously to move the ingredients towards the center of the sandwich with telekinesis.

Made for 1HGJ #238: Two Buttons

Socc-Arrr

Released on October 19, 2019

Bounce the soccer ball on the pirate ship as many times as you can before the ball falls into the water.

Made for 1HGJ #234: Pirates

Cook the Bacon on the Xbox, But Don't Melt the Xbox

Released on September 28, 2019

There's a very narrow temperature window between cooking the bacon and melting the Xbox. Be careful!

Made for 1HGJ #231: Overheating

Avant-Garde Applesauce Simulator

Released on September 21, 2019

The apples are falling off the shelf! Squish them with your shopping cart to make applesauce. There's no end goal besides filling up the whole screen with applesauce.

Made for 1HGJ #230: Shop

Halloween Bounce House 2

Released on September 14, 2019

It's like the first Halloween Bounce House, except there are now two halloween bounce houses. Move the grey ghost left and right to hit a purple ghost and cause the biggest chain reaction possible!

Made for 1HGJ #229: Halloween

Taco Tomagotchi

Released on September 7, 2019

Play with your new pet taco! Feed them to increase their happiness, or drop blocks at them to make them unhappy. You can change their color and location, and move them around.

Made for 1HGJ #228: Virtual Pet

Color Guesser

Released on August 17, 2019

Use the sliders to change the shape's color to match the background. Color as many shapes as possible in three minutes!

Made for 1HGJ #225: Colors

there are definitely trains in this game

Released on August 10, 2019

Move the trains around, and make them collide to reveal the picture underneath the screen. There's no real objective to this game.

Made for 1HGJ #224: Trains

Demonic Text

Released on August 3, 2019

Type the letters to make them bounce. Can you make the text DEMONIC?

Made for 1HGJ #223: Demons

Escape the Grapes

Released on July 27, 2019

Rover lost their tennis ball in a bucket of grapes! Use Rover's tongue to scoop out the grapes and rescue the tennis ball.

Made for 1HGJ #222: Escape

Inject to Disinfect

Released on July 20, 2019

Oh no, someone wants to eat an infected burger! Use your telekinetic powers to control the plate so that the burger gets injected with enough disinfectant before the person eats it.

Made for 1HGJ #221: Infection

Cheese Eater

Released on July 13, 2019

Eat that cheese to build those strong leg muscles! How many cheeses can you eat?

Made for 1HGJ #220: Growth

3D Foggy Drive

Released on July 6, 2019

Drive around the area, collecting fuel cans to increase the limit of your car's speed. Don't hit the cones or barriers. Can you collect all 50 cans?

Made for 1HGJ #219: Limit

Playing a Janky Version of Breakout on a CRT TV Sitting Behind a Mirror in the Bathroom of a Gourmet Restaurant at 2:19 PM

Released on June 22, 2019

Made for 1HGJ #217: Mirror

Mitosis Bowling

Released on June 15, 2019

It's like bowling, but the pins split into two identical pins whenever your ball hits them.

Made for 1HGJ #216: Clone

Golfing Through the Planets

Released on June 8, 2019

Infinite golf on infinite planets. Click to aim your shot, and release to shoot the ball. If a hole is impossible, press space to skip it, but you'll take a 10 stroke penalty.

Made for 1HGJ #215: Not On Earth

Synchronized Lawn Mowers On Ice

Released on June 1, 2019

Pull off the performance of a lifetime by drifting to score points! You control both lawn mowers simultaneously. Don't hit anything!

Made for 1HGJ #214: Ice (and also Lawn mowers)

Yee Haw

Released on May 25, 2019

The people want cowboy hats! They'll chase after your hat, and shout yee haw once they get it. If they bump into someone else, they'll create a new person.

Made for 1HGJ #213: Overpopulation

Anti-Eraser

Released on May 18, 2019

Use the anti-eraser to un-erase the whiteboard!

Made for 1HGJ #212: Invisible

The Running Man

Released on May 11, 2019

This man has three legs! Run through the randomly generated levels and try to get the best time.

Made for 1HGJ #211: Running

Orange you glad I didn't throw banana?

Released on April 6, 2019

Toss the orange as far as you can! Use the left and right arrow keys to control the banana.

Made for 1HGJ #206: Banana

Ball to the Walls

Released on March 16, 2019

Hit the walls to earn points. The more points you earn, the faster you can roll the ball. Get as many points as possible in one minute!

Made for 1HGJ #203: one hard minute

flip/flop

Released on February 23, 2019

You control two cubes. They move in opposite directions. Move and jump through the level to make them touch each other.

Made for 1HGJ #200: Reflection

Potaxi

Released on February 16, 2019

The Potaxis are potatos that are also taxis. They go until they crash. How many Potaxis can you have on screen?

Made for 1HGJ #199: Automation

there are definitely no trains in this game

Released on February 2, 2019

Hit the boxes with your UFO to reveal a car. Hit some cars into each other, and you'll get a new method of transportation. Keep going, and you eventually will not find any trains, because there are no trains in this game.

Made for 1HGJ #197: Misleading title
